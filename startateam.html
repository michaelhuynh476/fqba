Advancement

All our matches use NAQT Invitational sets for questions, providing the best practice for those looking to participate in tournaments such as HSNCT (High School National Championship Tournament). Teams can also qualify for HSNCT at FQBA events, a significant achievement for any team.

What is Quizbowl?

Quizbowl is an exciting academic competition where teams of students compete to answer questions about various subjects such as history, science, literature, and general knowledge. Participants use buzzers to buzz in and answer questions, testing their knowledge and quick thinking skills.

How Does Quizbowl Work?

In Quizbowl matches, a moderator reads questions to the competing teams. Team members compete to buzz in first and correctly answer the questions. Points are awarded for correct answers, and there are often different rounds and formats used in competitions.

Start a Quizbowl Team

  1. Find Interested Team Members: Gather a group of students who are passionate about quizbowl and willing to participate.
  2. Secure a Faculty Advisor: Approach a teacher or staff member who can serve as the team's advisor. They can provide guidance and support.
  3. Research Tournaments: Explore local quizbowl tournaments and competitions where your team can participate. Look for dates, locations, and registration details.
  4. Arrange Practice Sessions: Schedule regular practice sessions to prepare your team. Use quizbowl questions to simulate tournament conditions.
  5. Register for Tournaments: Register your team for upcoming tournaments. Pay attention to registration deadlines and fees.
  6. Prepare for Tournaments: Study various subjects, practice buzzer skills, and review quizbowl rules to ensure your team is well-prepared for competitions.
  7. Participate and Have Fun: Attend tournaments, compete, and enjoy the quizbowl experience. Celebrate your team's achievements and continue learning from each competition.
