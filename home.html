What is Quizbowl?

Quiz bowl is a competitive academic activity in which teams of students compete to answer questions about various topics, including general knowledge, history, literature, science, fine arts, current events, and more. It is typically played with two teams, each consisting of several players. A moderator asks questions, and the teams try to buzz in and answer correctly to earn points for their team.

Florida Quizbowl Association

The mission of the FQBA is to provide organized, state-wide tournaments and packets to the entirety of Florida, and in so doing foster the growth of learning in all interested students!
